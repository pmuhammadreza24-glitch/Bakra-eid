Celebrate Eid ul Adha

Share happiness and help needy families during this blessed occasion.

bakra eid

Bakra Eid, also known as Eid ul Adha, is celebrated on the 10th of Dhul Hijjah, the final month of the Islamic lunar calendar.

Qurbani Distribution

Muslims distribute Qurbani meat among family, friends, and poor people to spread happiness and unity during Eid ul Adha.

Eid Prayer

On Eid ul Adha, Muslims gather in mosques and open grounds to offer Eid prayer and thank Allah for His blessings.
